2-Information collected through technology;
I. Our servers can collect the type of your mobile device or computer, the information of your mobile application and browser, your IP address and related technological information and data. II. The websites you visit can save small information on your computer or phone and then read that information when you visit the same site. The files in which this small information is saved are called cookies or definition information. Our application can access cookie data.

Face Data — Maxxing app

12a. Scope. The provisions in this section apply only to the Maxxing app.

12b. What we collect. When you submit a photo through Maxxing, the image is processed solely to generate your facial-feature reading and editorial sketch. We do not extract biometric identifiers (such as faceprints) from the image and do not match it against any database.

12c. How we collect it. Only after you tap “Add a photo” inside the Maxxing app and explicitly accept the in-app data-sharing consent prompt. No background or passive collection occurs.

12d. Who we share it with. To produce the reading, Maxxing transmits the photo to OpenAI (api.openai.com) for the textual analysis and fal.ai for the editorial sketch. These are the only third parties that receive the image. Each provider operates under its own privacy policy and is contractually required to apply data-protection standards equivalent to those described in this policy.

12e. Retention. The image is held only transiently while the analysis runs and is not persisted to any disk or database on our servers. The resulting report and sketch are stored locally on your device only. Deleting the app removes all local data.

12f. Your rights. Deleting the app removes all local data and revokes consent for future submissions. For any data-related requests, see the contact details in the Contact section below.
